About Qustezle Mini Games

I'm passionate about making websites that are both fun and useful. I enjoy creating websites. And completing a project is the best feeling. I wanted to create some other projects; I have the idea but the projects that I want to build required backend, which I don't know. So, I want to learn backend and improve my frontend skills.

What Is Qustezle Mini Games?

Qustezle Mini Games is a web-based collection of simple, entertaining mini games you can play instantly. You don't need to downlaod anything, play directly in your browser. This project was created as for my solo project for joining Chingu voyages, a platform for gaining real world job experience for developers. Qustezle offers quick, interactive fun and is designed to be user-friendly and it's accessible to everyone. I tried my best to make it an attractive website.

Website Features

I listed the features of my website here. The features are listed below:

  • Variety of Mini Games: Just hit “Start Playing” to explore different kinds of games (new games added over time!).
  • Clean, Modern Design: The site is built with HTML and CSS for a smooth, visually appealing experience.
  • Contact Form: Have questions, ideas, or want to say something? Use the contact page! Your messages are sent directly to my email.
  • Feedback System: Share your thoughts or rate the website through the feedback page. Your feedback goes directly to me and this feedback will help me improve Qustezle.
  • Essential Site Pages: Includes Home, About, Contact, Feedback, Privacy Policy, Terms and Conditions, and Instructions for easy navigation and transparency.
  • Working Forms:All forms on my website work properly. The information from the form reaches to me directly. I have used Web3Forms for this.
