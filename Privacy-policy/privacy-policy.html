Privacy policy

Last updated 1 August 2025

This website is a simple mini games website created as a Chingu solo project.

Information Collection

This website doesn't collect any user data, such as IP addresses, usage information, or store, or share any personal Information of the users. You don't have to login or create an account; no personal data submission required to play the mini games.
The only time I collect information is when you use the contact form. To ensure I can reply to you, I collect the name and email address you provide. This information is only used to reply to your message. I don't save, share or sell this data.

Cookies and Tracking

This website doesn't use any cookies or any tracking technologies.

Third Parties

The website doesn't share any user's information with third parties.
The website's contact form is powered by Web3Forms. They help send the form data to me. You can read their privacy policy on their website for more details on how they handle form submissions. Please note that I am not responsible for the privacy practices of Web3Forms.

Contact

If you have any questions about this website, please feel free to contact me. You can contact me by submitting the form on the Contact page of the website. I will reply to you as soon as possible.
